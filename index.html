About Me

I am a self taught frontend developer with proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I grew up in Dover, Pennsylvania and had gained the passion for design at a young age. I would love art class in elementary and was excited when I found the graphic communication classes my high school had to offer. Around sophomore year I would discover UI/UX design and start improving my design skills. I thought that UI/UX was interesting, but I was more interested in actually being able to code and put websites/applications out to the world. So I started to learn how to code, and after many doubtful attempts, I managed to understand it.

My love for design does not end there though. I had also taken marketing classes in high school and enjoyed coming up with different brands. Picking out color palettes, Typography, Tone, Graphic styles and everything that went into the personality of a brand was fun for me.

The combination of these classes that sparked passion in my life are what fuel my creative decisions. I like for every website I design to be personalized for that brand and how they want to have the viewer feel. Without branding, the world of commerce would be boring and bland. So I plan on using my design skills to make the client a personalized, beautiful, and useful site that will last for a lifetime.
